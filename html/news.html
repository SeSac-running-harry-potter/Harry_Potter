The Dumbledore Scandal
In the book series "Harry Potter" written by J.K. Rowling, it is revealed that Albus Dumbledore, the headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, is gay. J.K. Rowling made this revelation after the publication of the final book in the series, "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows." She stated in interviews and on social media that she always envisioned Dumbledore as a gay character and that he had a romantic relationship with the dark wizard Gellert Grindelwald during their youth. This aspect of Dumbledore's character, however, is not explicitly mentioned or explored in the main series, but rather confirmed by the author outside of the books.
